FINAL PROMPT — Food Safety Officer Action Dashboard

Role: You are a senior government e-governance product architect and compliance-driven dashboard designer.

Objective: Design a centralized Action & Reminder Dashboard for Food Safety Officers that ensures no statutory, legal, or administrative action is missed.

1️⃣ Dashboard Purpose

The dashboard must act as a daily command center for Food Safety Officers by:

Showing action-required items

Sending timely reminders

Tracking pending, overdue, and completed actions

Supporting legal, enforcement, laboratory, and administrative workflows

2️⃣ Action Categories (Mandatory)

The dashboard must support the following action-required categories:

Legal & Court

Court cases

Adjudication files to be filed

Penalties

Inspections & Enforcement

Follow-up inspections

Inspections pending

Improvement notices

Seized articles

Destroyed articles

Sampling & Laboratory

Samples pending

Lab reports received

Lab reports not received

Unsafe samples

Sub-standard samples

Misbranded samples

Schedule IV cases

Administrative & Compliance

Special drives

Workshops & trainings

DLAC meetings

FSSAI initiatives

Protocol & Special Duties

VVIP / ASL protocol duties

3️⃣ Dashboard Structure

Design the dashboard with:

Action Cards / Tiles (one per category)

Each card must display:

Total items

Pending

Overdue

Due today / this week

Priority Indicators

Critical (legal / unsafe / court-linked)

High

Normal

4️⃣ Reminder & Alert Logic

The system must support:

Auto-generated reminders based on:

Due dates

Statutory timelines

Lab report SLA

Court / adjudication deadlines

Reminder modes:

In-app notification

Dashboard highlight

Escalation if overdue

5️⃣ Action Drill-Down

Clicking any dashboard item should open:

Full case / inspection / sample details

Current status

Action history

Next required action

Responsible officer

6️⃣ Status Lifecycle

Each action item must support:

Pending

In Progress

Completed

Overdue

With immutable audit logs for all changes.

7️⃣ Personalization & Filters

Allow officers to:

View My Actions

Filter by:

Category

Priority

Date range

Status

8️⃣ Backend & Compliance Expectations

Statutory deadline awareness

Role-based visibility

Offline support with sync

Audit-ready logs

Future reporting & export support

9️⃣ Expected Output

Provide:

Dashboard layout & component structure

Action categorization model

Reminder & escalation logic

Data model & APIs

Edge cases & compliance safeguards

Design for real-world government workflows, legal accountability, and zero-miss compliance.
